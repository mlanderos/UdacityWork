Come see Link you have never seen him before. This game is the largest installment within the entire Legend of Zelda series. You embark on a journey across many different areas; mountains, forests, villages, deserts, caves, seas & more! You can walk. You can ride. You can glide. You can climb. You can swim. Thankfully, you can transport to your required destinations to fulfill your destiny.

Or

You can put aside the main quest and do what Link enjoys most. FISH of course! Break pots in hopes to find money and fill your pockets. Play mini-games to win cool unrequired items. Because I really need those fashionable boots to complete my journey!
Which path will you choose?
